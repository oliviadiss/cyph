03 — Principles

Space functions as punctuation

01

Darkness as substrate

The navy-black field is not background. It is the encrypted, the undisclosed — the work before it resolves into public form.

02

Legible power

Every form is precise by intent. Each proportion is a deliberate argument, calibrated to hold authority across every scale.

03

Dual valence

Violet carries the electric hum of technology; amber the settled weight of legal tradition. The gradient earns the space between.

04

Emit, don't reflect

Mark and wordmark glow against the substrate. The emblem carries its references lightly and rewards scrutiny.

04 — Illustration · client-supplied

The illustrations do not fit the system

These portraits were supplied as the look the client wants. Placed against the brand as built, they break it. The frames below load illustration-1.png and illustration-2.png from the same folder as this page.

Reference 1 · illustration-1.png

Cartoon caricature, broad grin, holographic practice-area graphics, framed awards, a trophy.

Reference 2 · illustration-2.png

Glowing AI hero render, gavel and IP-law book, gold "Patent Star" trophy, tech-lit skyline.

  • 1
    Serif restraint vs cartoon energyThe system is Cormorant Garamond on near-black: quiet, timeless, editorial. The illustrations are loud, busy, and grinning. Opposite registers.
  • 2
    Off-palette colourBright cyan holograms, gold trophies, a red tie, blue glow. None of it belongs to the indigo, amber, and obsidian palette.
  • 3
    Self-promotion vs quiet authorityBoth images foreground the man and his awards. The brand foregrounds the mark and restraint. Trophy-flaunting undercuts the seniority the system signals.
  • 4
    Two different styles, no languageOne is a flat caricature, the other a photoreal render. They do not even agree with each other, so there is no single illustration system to adopt.
  • 5
    AI-render finishWarped text, invented award names, and dreamy lighting read as AI-generated. On a law firm site that erodes trust rather than building it.
Recommendation. Do not add these to the system. If a human element is needed, use restrained duotone photography on the obsidian field, or a single line-based illustration language drawn from the C lettermark. Both keep the authority the brand is built on.
